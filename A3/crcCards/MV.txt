Class name: MV
Parent Class: Command
Subclasses: 

Responsibilities:
Move item OLDPATH to NEWPATH. [mv OLDPATH NEWPATH]
Both OLDPATH and NEWPATH may be relative to the current directory or 
may be full paths. 
If NEWPATH is a directory, move the item into the directory.
OLDPATH will be removed
