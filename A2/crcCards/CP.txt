Class name: CP
Parent Class: Command
Subclasses: 

Responsibilities:
Copy item OLDPATH to NEWPATH. [cp OLDPATH NEWPATH]
Both OLDPATH and NEWPATH may be relative to the current directory or 
may be full paths. 
If OLDPATH is a directory, copy the all of its contents as well.
OLDPATH will not be removed
